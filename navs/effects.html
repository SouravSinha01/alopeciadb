Effects of Androgenic Alopecia
Physical Effects

Androgenic alopecia causes hair thinning and loss, leading to various psychological effects such as reduced self-esteem and social anxiety.

Psychological Impact

Psychological effects of alopecia include a sense of reduced confidence and social anxiety due to visible hair loss.
